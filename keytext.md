# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Never miss a contract deadline. AI-powered tracking, smart reminders, and document scanning in one app.

## Description
Stop letting contracts auto-renew without your knowledge. Stop missing notice deadlines that cost you money. ContractGuard is the smart contract tracker that keeps you ahead of every deadline.

Unlike basic reminder apps, ContractGuard understands the difference between an expiration date and a notice deadline. It knows you need to act weeks or months before a contract ends, and it tells you exactly when.

SMART URGENCY DASHBOARD
See all your contracts at a glance, sorted by urgency. Color-coded badges show you instantly which contracts need attention now and which are safe. No more digging through spreadsheets or email folders.

AI-POWERED EXTRACTION
Scan or upload a contract document and let AI do the work. ContractGuard automatically extracts key dates, renewal terms, payment details, and important clauses. No more manual data entry.

MULTI-TIMEPOINT REMINDERS
Get alerts at 90, 60, 30, 7, and 1 day(s) before your notice deadline. Never be caught off guard again. Each reminder gives you time to review, negotiate, or cancel.

DOCUMENT SCANNING
Use your iPhone or iPad camera to scan paper contracts. Built-in OCR technology digitizes your documents so you always have a searchable copy.

ICLOUD SYNC
Keep your contracts updated across all your Apple devices. Enable iCloud sync in Settings and your data follows you everywhere, securely encrypted.

KEY FEATURES
- Track unlimited contracts with automatic urgency levels
- AI-powered clause extraction from scanned documents
- Multi-timepoint deadline reminders (90/60/30/7/1 days)
- Notice period tracking separate from expiration dates
- Document scanning with OCR text recognition
- iCloud sync across all Apple devices
- Contract value and payment tracking
- Auto-renewal detection and alerts
- Search and filter by vendor, type, or urgency
- Archive completed contracts
