Privacy Policy for ContractGuard

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ContractGuard is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Contract Data: All contract information you enter (titles, vendor names, dates, values, notes) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.
  • AI Processing: When you use the AI extraction feature, contract text is sent to the Claude API (Anthropic) for analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy governs this data. We do not store or transmit this data to our own servers.
  • Subscription Data: Payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status, never your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the ContractGuard app
  • To send you contract deadline notifications you have requested
  • To process in-app purchases through Apple's secure payment system
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All contract data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Optional iCloud sync uses Apple's CloudKit with end-to-end encryption
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information
  • No contract data is stored on our servers

4. Third-Party Services

ContractGuard uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple CloudKit: For optional iCloud synchronization. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API: For AI-powered contract clause extraction. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchases. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

  • You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app
  • You can opt out of iCloud sync in the app settings
  • You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings
  • You can request data deletion by contacting us

6. Children's Privacy

ContractGuard is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
