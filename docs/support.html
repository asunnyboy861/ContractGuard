Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new contract?
Tap the "+" button on the Dashboard screen. Fill in the contract details including title, vendor, dates, and notice period. You can also scan a document using the Scan tab.
What does the notice period mean?
The notice period is the number of days before your contract expires that you need to take action (cancel, renew, or renegotiate). ContractGuard calculates your notice deadline automatically and sends you reminders.
How do notifications work?
ContractGuard sends reminders at 90, 60, 30, 7, and 1 day(s) before your notice deadline. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device Settings for ContractGuard.
How does AI extraction work?
Premium users can scan or upload contract documents. Our AI technology analyzes the text and automatically extracts key information like dates, renewal terms, payment terms, and important clauses.
Does ContractGuard sync across devices?
Yes! Premium users can enable iCloud sync in Settings to keep their contracts updated across all Apple devices signed into the same Apple ID.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > ContractGuard. Cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date.
Is my contract data secure?
Yes. All contract data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, data is encrypted and transmitted securely via Apple's CloudKit. We never store your contract data on our servers.

Troubleshooting

  • Not receiving notifications: Check Settings > Notifications > ContractGuard on your device
  • iCloud sync not working: Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices and iCloud Drive is enabled
  • Scanner not working: Ensure camera permissions are granted in Settings > Privacy > Camera
  • AI extraction failing: Check your internet connection and ensure the document text is clear and readable
